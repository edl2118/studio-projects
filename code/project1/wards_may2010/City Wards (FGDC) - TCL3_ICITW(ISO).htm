Language of Dataset: en
Abstract: The City electoral themes are a nested set. City Wards are built up from Voting Subdivisions which in turn are built up from City Blocks formed by the linear features of TCL. All addresses and linear features are implicitly linked to city blocks and therefore to all of City Wards and Voting Subdivisions. City Wards in total cover the whole city, they do not overlap each other.
Purpose: The City electoral themes aid the administration of the City electoral process. They can be used for aggregation, display, analysis, reporting, decision support of program activities and statistics.
Supplemental Information: Geospatial Viewing Repository running Oracle 9 and SDE3 on a Sun with Solaris operating system.
